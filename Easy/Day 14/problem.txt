Given two strings s1 and s2, you have to find the index of the first occurence of s2 in s1.
If s2 is not present in s1 then print -1.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Input:
The first and only line of input contains two strings s1 and s2 (1 <= s1.size(), s2.size() <=10^4).

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Output:
Print the index of the first occurence of s2 in s1.
Else print -1.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Time Limit: 1 sec
